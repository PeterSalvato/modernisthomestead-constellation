Modernist Homestead

Zero-Willpower Household Systems

The family's needs became more complex over time. ADHD means working memory is a scarce resource. Autism means sensory regulation is architecture, not preference. Celiac means a contamination isn't an inconvenience — it's a medical event. And willpower runs out by 5 PM.

Improvisation stopped working. You can't ask people to remember everything when remembering is exactly the thing they're constrained from doing. The environment has to do it.

Culinary modularity. Sensory zoning. Executive function scaffolding. Gamification. The system does the thinking when thinking is exactly what's constrained.
